About Me

Born and raised in the Mid-west. At the age of 6 I was asked what I wanted to be when he grew up? And for some unfathomable reason I said I wanted to be a cobbler. I have no idea why I said it or how I even knew what a cobbler was. Obviously my aspirations to mend shoes for a living was short lived. At the end of high school my love for film and tv led me to the idea of becoming a special effects technician. Sadly, this idea was also shot down after I found out that job security and pay of technicians wasn't the best. (Also I would have to move across the country and 18 year old me wasn't ready for that just yet.) At 21 I decided to follow in my father's footsteps and become a firefighter paramedic. I went to paramedic school and worked as an EMT-P for approximately 8 years. I very much enjoyed my job and thought I was set. Unfortunately fate is a cruel mistress and in 2019 I started having medical issues that were not conducive with the nature of being an EMT, and forced me to leave the healthcare field. Unsure of what to do next I took a seasonal job at a Amazon distrbution center that had just opened near my home to hold myself over for a few months until I figured out what to do next. . . that was 3 years ago. Then in June, 2023, 33 years old me was death scrolling through Amazon's career choices and I stumble on Correlation One's Software Developer program, I had taken a intro to HTML and CSS course in college and enjoyed it and I thought, hey this could be a good career path for me. Which brings us to the here and now enrolled in said software developer program. (And enjoying it might I add.) I'm happy to be here and look forward to seeing where this career path will take me.
